Cookie Policy

This cookie policy provides information about cookies and similar technologies used on the Kuzamo website. Cookies are used to provide you with a better experience on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files sent to your browser by the website. These files are stored on your computer and help the website recognize you, remember your preferences, and improve the overall user experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

🔒 Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the basic functions of the website and are used for security, session management, and basic site functionality.

  • Security and authentication
  • Session management
  • Form data processing
  • Basic site functionality

Note: These cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the website to function.

📊 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us analyze site usage, measure performance, and improve user experience.

  • Page view statistics
  • User behavior analysis
  • Site performance measurement
  • Error reporting

Note: These cookies are optional and are not used without your consent.

🎯 Marketing Cookies

These cookies help us provide you with personalized ads and content.

  • Targeted advertisements
  • Content personalization
  • Marketing campaigns
  • Conversion tracking

Note: These cookies are optional and are not used without your consent.

⚙️ Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your language preferences, theme choices, and other personalization settings.

  • Language preferences
  • Theme choices (light/dark)
  • User settings
  • Personalization preferences

Note: These cookies are optional and are not used without your consent.

Cookie Management

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time:

Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner that allows you to accept all cookies or customize your preferences.

⚙️ Cookie Settings

You can access detailed cookie settings through the floating widget (bottom-right corner) or by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the banner.

🔄 Update Preferences

You can update your cookie preferences at any time by revisiting the cookie settings modal.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services:

📊 Analytics Services

  • Google Analytics for website performance
  • Performance monitoring tools
  • Error tracking services

🌐 Social Media Platforms

  • LinkedIn integration cookies
  • X (Twitter) sharing cookies
  • Instagram embedding cookies

Cookie Duration

Cookie Lifespan:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain until expiration date or manual deletion
  • Necessary Cookies: Automatically renewed for site functionality

Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

🔧 Browser Controls

  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set cookie preferences

📱 Mobile Devices

  • iOS Safari settings
  • Android Chrome settings
  • Mobile app preferences
  • Privacy settings

  • Changes will be posted on this page
  • Significant changes will be notified via email
  • Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes
